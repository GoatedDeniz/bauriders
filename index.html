01 / What We Ride For

MORE THAN
A CLUB.

Three things define us. They show up on every ride, at every Tuesday, in every new member we welcome.

/ 01

THE RIDES

Weekly group rides in and around Istanbul. Routes we've memorized, and some we're still discovering. Riders of every level, one formation.

/ 02

THE COMMUNITY

Students, alumni, and new riders — all welcome. Fantastic Tuesdays, mechanic workshops, safety clinics, and plans that somehow always end with a ride.

/ 03

Safety first, always. No one gets left behind. We represent BAU on every ride — and we leave every road better than we found it.

02 / Our Signature Night

FANTASTIC
TUESDAYS.

Every Tuesday — rain, shine, or somewhere in between — we gather for the one night that defines BAU Riders.

19:00
PUB SESSION
We meet, we unwind, we swap ride stories, we welcome new faces.
LATER
ENGINES ON
Helmets on. We ride in formation to the week's selected café.
~23:00

Coffee, conversation, debates about which route is actually the fastest.
THE ARTÇI RULE

No motorbike? No problem. Ride as an Artçı (passenger) on the back of a member's bike — as long as a helmet is available or you bring your own. No helmet, no ride.

01 / Our Story

BORN ON
CAMPUS.
FORGED ON
THE ROAD.

BAU Riders is the official motorbiking community of Bahçeşehir University — a brotherhood and sisterhood of students, alumni, and passionate riders united by two wheels and a love for the open road.

What started as a handful of students meeting between classes has grown into one of Istanbul's most active university rider communities. We ride together, we learn together, and we look out for each other on every kilometer.

Our Mission

"To build a safe, skilled, and tight-knit community of student riders — and to make motorbiking accessible to every BAU student who wants in."

04 / The Pipeline

BAU RIDERS
JUNIOR TEAM.

The Junior Team is our training ground for future leadership — a trial board that gets hands-on experience running the club before earning a seat on the main YK.

Juniors shadow the YK, co-lead rides, help organize Fantastic Tuesdays, and take the lead on smaller events. It's where next year's board is built — one kilometer at a time.

If you see yourself leading BAU Riders one day, the Junior Team is your on-ramp.
